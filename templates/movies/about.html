About CinePick

Helping movie nights feel more personal and less random

CinePick was built to make choosing a movie easier. Instead of endless scrolling, we bring together search, curated genres, AI-assisted suggestions, and your personal wishlist so you can move from “what should we watch?” to “let’s start this one.”

What we do

We turn movie discovery into a calmer, cleaner experience. CinePick helps you explore titles, revisit ideas you want to watch later, and quickly narrow down choices based on mood or genre.

Discover Faster Browse random picks, search for specific titles, or jump into genre-based collections.
Save What Matters Use your wishlist to keep track of films you do not want to lose between sessions.
Use AI Naturally Describe a vibe or story type and let CinePick translate that into movie suggestions.
